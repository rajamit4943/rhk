HOW TO BUY

Create a Wallet

download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Get Some WPLS

have WPLS in your wallet to switch to $KING. If you dont have any WPLS, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to 9inch

connect to 9inch. Go to app.9inch.io in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $KING token address into 9inch, select KING, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Swap WPLS for $KING

Swap WPLS for $KING. We have ZERO taxes so you dont need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
